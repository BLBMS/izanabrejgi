Description

Return to the times of your grandparents in a pleasant little house in a quiet location among vineyards, but in a modern way with all the comforts of today.

Fall asleep in silence and in the moonlight, wake up with birdsong.

You can enjoy nature in your own yard, explore the surroundings on foot or by bicycle on marked trails, or also enjoy the nearby thermal baths.

The translation from Prekmurje dialect is "House on the Hill". The word "hill" is not used locally, instead the word "breg" is used, which means small hill, or dialectally "brejg". For the inhabitants of Spodnje Moravče (this is the lower, flatter part of present-day Moravske Toplice), the first elevation in the hinterland of the village was primarily useful for vineyards. The more flat areas near farms were used as agricultural land.

The first mention of the house dates back to 1864 and is already marked on the first maps. The original purpose of the building was a vineyard cottage, where workers lived with their families and took care of these vineyards. The vineyards and buildings were owned by wealthier farmers from the lower part of the village.

The house is furnished with restored stylish furniture from that time, and many pieces exceed the venerable age of 100 years.

We have made an effort to take you on a journey into the past, so you can take time for yourself and your loved ones and perhaps also reflect on former values that are disappearing more and more in this fast-paced world.

Please use the house and furniture carefully and attentively, so that we can pass it on to our grandchildren and great-grandchildren in the next 100 years in the same condition as it was passed on to us by our ancestors.
